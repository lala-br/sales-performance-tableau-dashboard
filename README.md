# Sales Performance Dashboard (Tableau)

## Introduction
This project focuses on building interactive Tableau dashboards to help stakeholders — including sales managers and executives — analyze sales performance and customer trends.

The dashboards provide insights into year-over-year sales performance, monthly and weekly trends, and product subcategory comparisons to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Dashboard Purpose
The purpose of the Sales Performance Dashboard is to present a clear overview of key sales metrics and trends, enabling stakeholders to:
- Compare current year vs previous year performance
- Identify high- and low-performing periods
- Analyze sales and profit across product subcategories

---

## Key Requirements & Implementation

### 1. KPI Overview
- Displays total **Sales**, **Profit**, and **Quantity**
- Compares **current year** vs **previous year**
- Enables quick high-level performance evaluation

---

### 2. Sales Trends (Monthly)
- Monthly breakdown of:
  - Sales
  - Profit
  - Quantity
- Comparison between current year and previous year
- Highlights:
  - Highest-performing months
  - Lowest-performing months
- Visual cues make trends easy to interpret at a glance

---

### 3. Product Subcategory Comparison
- Compares sales performance across product subcategories
- Side-by-side comparison for:
  - Current year
  - Previous year
- Includes profit comparison to evaluate profitability alongside revenue

---

### 4. Weekly Trends for Sales & Profit
- Weekly sales and profit analysis for the current year
- Displays:
  - Average weekly sales
  - Average weekly profit
- Highlights weeks:
  - Above average
  - Below average
- Helps identify short-term performance fluctuations

---
